About Us

This website provides an e-commerce platfrom for Online Book Library and Book Sale.
It provides the user with a catalog of different books available for Online Reading as a Library Service and also purchase the hard-copy of the book from the store.In order to facilitate online purchase a shopping cart is provided to the user.
In order to provide Library Service we are providing various membership categories (like Trial, Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly and Yearly).
Membership Type will decide the nominal Subscription Fees.
A single member will be able to subscribe 4 books for a week.
